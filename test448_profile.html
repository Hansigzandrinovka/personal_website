Born 21 B.E. to Dr. Elain and Major Adam Fenix

Markus' rough childhood was magnified by the death of his mother, creating a divide and at the same time a connection between him and his father.

Known for his gruff and blunt attitude, Major Fenix has always had trouble with authority.

Despite this, he knuckles down when he needs to and gets the job done.

Markus is considered a legendary hero among the people of the Coalition of Ordered Governments despite being branded a coward for leaving his post during the Battle of Ephyra

Markus has an almost inhuman hatred of the Locust Horde, which has served him well as a Gear, but has also gotten him into situations he's barely made it out of alive.
